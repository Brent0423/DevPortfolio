About Me

I am a dedicated self-taught software engineer with a significant background in financial planning and sales. Starting with a degree in Finance, I worked as a licensed advisor for four years in the financial planning sector, where my deep dive into data analysis sparked a burgeoning interest in technology and efficiency. This journey then led me to spend the next four years as a sales professional in the grand format printing industry, where my involvement in sales automation further fueled my desire for technological innovation and efficient processes. These experiences, combined with the inspiring tales of creativity and satisfaction of project completion from my cousin, a lead developer, ignited a passion within me for the world of software engineering.


Leveraging my diverse skills and interests, I've brought life to an array of interesting projects. These include a data-driven financial tool for ROI predictions, a geolocation-based app for ideal solar eclipse viewing, and a comprehensive tool ranking US ski resorts on snow conditions.


Proficient in Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and SQL, with a working knowledge of Java and hands-on experience using Flask and Docker, I am driven to leverage my expertise in financial planning and sales, along with my software development skills, to create meaningful and impactful solutions. I eagerly look forward to bringing my unique combination of skills and experiences to a forward-thinking software engineering team.
